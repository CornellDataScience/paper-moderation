Jacek Graniecki (Polish pronunciation: [ˈjat͡sɛk ɡraˈɲɛt͡skʲi]), known professionally as Tede (Polish pronunciation: [ˈtɛdɛ]) and DJ Buhh (Polish pronunciation: [ˈdid͡ʐɛj ˈbux])  is a Polish rapper. Also known as Tas De Fleia, TDF, Tedzik, Tedunio, and Chory Pastor. He began his career in the band 1 kHz (Ein Killa Hertz). In 1996, JanMario and Tede created a band named Trzyha (Triple H, which stands for Hardcore Hip-Hop), with the group changing their name to Warszafski Deszcz (Warsaw's Rain) in 1998. In 2000, Tede began his solo career and in 2001 he recorded his first album (S.P.O.R.T). In 2002, he founded his own record label - Wielkie Joł. He was ranked third in a music magazine Machina toplist of thirty best Polish rappers in 2011.
