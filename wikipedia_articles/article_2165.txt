Lord of Higuera de Vargas (Spanish: Señor de Higuera de Vargas) is a hereditary title in the Peerage of Spain accompanied by the dignity of Grandee, granted in 1390 by John I to Alonso Fernández de Vargas. The title makes reference to Higuera de Vargas, a town in Badajoz.
