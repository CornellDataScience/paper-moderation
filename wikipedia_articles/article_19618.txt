Yasuo Muramatsu (Japanese: 村松 康雄, Hepburn: Muramatsu Yasuo, April 6, 1933 – April 11, 2024) was a Japanese actor, voice actor and narrator. He was a founder of Office Kaoru. Muramatsu died on April 11, 2024, at the age of 91.
