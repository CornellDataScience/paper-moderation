2nd Bezlesnoye or Vtoroye Bezlesnoye (Russian: 2-е Безлесное, Второе Безлесное) is a rural locality (Russian: деревня, lit. 'village') in Lebyazhensky Selsoviet Rural Settlement, Kursky District, Kursk Oblast, Russia. Population:  65 (2010 Census); 79 (2002 Census); 


== Geography ==
The village is located on the Mlodat River (a left tributary of the Seym), 87 km from the Russia–Ukraine border, 13 km south-east of Kursk, 2 km from the selsoviet center – Cheryomushki.

Climate
2nd Bezlesnoye has a warm-summer humid continental climate (Dfb in the Köppen climate classification).


== Transport ==
2nd Bezlesnoye is located 3.5 km from the road of intermunicipal significance 38H-416 (Kursk – Petrin), on the road 38H-417 (38N-416 – 2nd Bezlesnoye), 9 km from the nearest railway halt Zaplava (railway line Klyukva — Belgorod).
The rural locality is situated 18.5 km from Kursk Vostochny Airport, 106 km from Belgorod International Airport and 204 km from Voronezh Peter the Great Airport.
