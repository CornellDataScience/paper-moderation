This is a list of bridges and viaducts in Finland, including those for pedestrians and vehicular traffic.


== Historical and architectural interest bridges ==
The museum roads and bridges was established by the Finnish Road Administration (Finnish: Tiehallinto) that belongs to the Finnish Transport Infrastructure Agency, in order to preserve the most historically valuable and representative parts of the road network as museum objects. There is a total of 22 roads (35 kilometres (22 mi)) and 37 bridges in the museum object collection.
