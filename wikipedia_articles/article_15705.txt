Hiromi Konno (今野 宏美, Konno Hiromi, born September 13, 1975) is a Japanese voice actress and narrator from Hokkaido. She graduated from Faculty of Economics at Sapporo University. After leaving Marcus and Aoni-juku she is now affiliated with Aoni Production.
