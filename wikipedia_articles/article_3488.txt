Proletarovka (Russian: Пролетаровка) is a rural locality (a khutor) in Soldatsky Selsoviet Rural Settlement, Fatezhsky District, Kursk Oblast, Russia. The population as of 2010 is 12.


== Geography ==
The khutor is located on the Radubezhsky Brook (a link tributary of the Usozha in the basin of the Svapa), 86.5 km from the Russia–Ukraine border, 53 km north-west of Kursk, 16 km south-west of the district center – the town Fatezh, 9 km from the selsoviet center – Soldatskoye.


=== Climate ===
Proletarovka has a warm-summer humid continental climate (Dfb in the Köppen climate classification).


== Transport ==
Proletarovka is located 14.5 km from the federal route M-2 Crimea Highway as part of the European route E105, 5 km from the road of regional importance 38K-038 (Fatezh – Dmitriyev), 10 km from the road 38K-005 (Konyshyovka – Zhigayevo – 38K-038), 6.5 km from the road of intermunicipal significance 38Н-679 (38K-038 – Soldatskoye – Shuklino), 4 km from the road 38Н-681 (38N-679 – Verkhniye Khalchi), on the road 38Н-682 (38N-681 – Podymovka – Proletarovka), 23 km from the nearest railway halt Mitsen (railway line Arbuzovo – Luzhki-Orlovskiye).
The rural locality is situated 57 km from Kursk Vostochny Airport, 169 km from Belgorod International Airport and 250 km from Voronezh Peter the Great Airport.
